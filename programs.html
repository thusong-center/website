Programs that change lives

Arts & culture, sports & recreation, dialogue circles, parolee support, and homework facilities — meeting youth where they are.

Arts & culture

Drama/acting, traditional and contemporary dance, pottery, and music — engaging talents and offering safe alternatives to crime.

Sports & recreation

Squash, judo, fitness, and aerobics — accessible, healthy activities for adults, youth, women, and children.

Dialogue circle

Community dialogues with youth, parents, volunteers, and NGOs — addressing GBV, substance abuse, mental health, bullying, and more.

Parolees & former incarcerated

With Randburg Correctional Service and partners, we support rehabilitation, community service, mediation, and job readiness.

Homework facilities

Volunteers help learners read and write; over 6000 kids in Alexandra can now read and write through our efforts.
