About Thusong Youth Centre

Thusong Youth Centre has been empowering young people in Johannesburg for over a decade. Our mission is to provide digital literacy, career support, and creative media opportunities that unlock pathways to education and employment.

We work with local partners, mentors, and organizations to ensure every program is community-led and impactful. Our approach is practical, inclusive, and focused on real outcomes.

Impact highlights

  • 120+ active members engaged across multiple programs.
  • 15 ongoing programs and workshops throughout the year.
  • 10 years of sustained community service and growth.
