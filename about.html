About Thusong Youth Centre

Established in 1979 as a joint project of Alexandra and Sandton residents, serving youth with training, recreation, and culture.

Mission

We capacitate and develop disadvantaged youth through training, skills development, and holistic programs that build confidence, leadership, and opportunity.

Vision

To develop individual skills, self‑esteem, initiative, and education — enabling young people to thrive and contribute to a healthy community.

Aims and objectives

  • Supportive recreational and cultural facilities for youth.
  • Promote education, responsibility, and community spirit.
  • Establish leadership and welfare training facilities.
  • Advance spiritual, educational, physical, and social welfare.
  • Represent and sponsor the needs and voices of young people.
